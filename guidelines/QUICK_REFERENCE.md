## ðŸ  SELF-CARE

### Headache (mild, <72 hours, no red flags)
- Rest in dark, quiet room
- Hydration (8-10 glasses water/day)
- OTC: Acetaminophen 500-1000mg or Ibuprofen 400-600mg
- Cold compress
- Avoid triggers

**Follow up if**: Persists >3 days, worsens, new symptoms

### Nausea (mild, <24 hours, tolerating fluids)
- BRAT diet (bananas, rice, applesauce, toast)
- Clear fluids: water, electrolyte solution, ginger ale
- Small frequent sips
- Ginger tea/candies
- OTC: Bismuth subsalicylate (Pepto-Bismol)

**Follow up if**: Unable to keep fluids down 12 hours, persists >48 hours

### Dizziness (mild, <48 hours, no red flags)
- Hydration
- Avoid sudden position changes (stand slowly)
- Adequate nutrition
- Remove fall hazards
- **Do not drive** while dizzy

**Follow up if**: Persists >3 days, worsens, falls

### Fatigue (mild, <2 weeks, known cause)
- Sleep: 7-9 hours/night, consistent schedule
- Nutrition: balanced diet, hydration
- Activity: 30 min moderate exercise most days
- Stress management

**Follow up if**: Persists >2 weeks, worsens, new symptoms

## ðŸ“‹ KEY ASSESSMENT QUESTIONS

### Headache (OPQRST)
- **O**nset: When did it start? Sudden or gradual?
- **P**rovokes/Palliates: What makes it better/worse?
- **Q**uality: Throbbing, pressure, sharp, dull?
- **R**egion/Radiation: Where? Unilateral or bilateral?
- **S**everity: 0-10 scale?
- **T**iming: Constant or intermittent? How long?
- **Associated**: Nausea, vision changes, aura, fever?

### Nausea/Vomiting
- Onset and duration?
- Frequency (episodes/day)?
- Appearance of vomit (clear, food, bile, blood)?
- Can you keep fluids down?
- Abdominal pain? Location/severity?
- Fever, diarrhea, other symptoms?
- Recent food/exposures?

### Dizziness
- Type: Spinning (vertigo), lightheaded, off-balance?
- Onset: Sudden or gradual?
- Duration: Seconds, minutes, hours?
- Triggers: Positional, standing, head movement?
- Associated: Hearing changes, nausea, headache?
- Falls or near-falls?

### Fatigue
- Duration? Onset (sudden/gradual)?
- Severity: 0-10 scale?
- Pattern: Constant or worse at certain times?
- Functional impact: Work? Daily tasks?
- Sleep: Hours per night? Quality?
- Mood: Feeling down or anxious?
- Other symptoms: Fever, weight change, pain?

### Orthostatic Symptoms
- Symptoms with standing: Lightheaded, vision changes, weakness?
- How quickly after standing?
- Loss of consciousness?
- Falls or near-falls?
- Time of day worse (morning often worse)?
- Recent illness, vomiting, diarrhea?
- Medication changes?

## ðŸ“ž ESCALATION PATHWAYS

### High Urgency (Red Flags)
- **Action**: Call 911 immediately
- **Message**: "Based on your symptoms, you need immediate medical attention. Please call 911 or go to the nearest emergency department."
- **Do NOT**: Schedule appointment, delay care, recommend self-care

### Urgent (Same-day)
- **Action**: Same-day appointment (within 24 hours)
- **Options**: 
  - In-person visit (preferred for vitals, exam)
  - Urgent care if primary care unavailable
  - Nurse line for assessment
- **Message**: "You should be seen today. I'll connect you with [scheduling/nurse line]."

### Routine (1 week)
- **Action**: Standard appointment scheduling
- **Options**:
  - Telehealth initial visit (if appropriate)
  - In-person follow-up if needed
- **Message**: "Let's schedule an appointment within the next week to evaluate your symptoms."

### Self-Care
- **Action**: Provide self-care instructions + follow-up plan
- **Include**:
  - Specific recommendations
  - Warning signs to watch for
  - Timeline to follow up (if not better)
- **Safety net**: "If symptoms worsen or you develop [red flags], seek immediate care."
